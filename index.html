Minecraft Crystals Collector

  • You will be given a random number to match at the start of the game.

  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your running total.

  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. You must identify it by how it changes your running total and then commit it to memory.

  • You win the game by matching your running total to the random number that you are trying to match. You lose the game if your total score goes above that same random number.

  • The value of each of the crystals will randomly change with the start of each turn.
