Minecraft Resource Dispenser

  • You will be given a number to match, which represents the total weight of an order for resources that you need to fill. Your job is use the resources to collect the exact weight that the order asks for.

  • There are four resource items below. By clicking on a resource you will add a specific amount of weight to your bag.

  • The weight of each resource item is unknown until you click on it. You must identify its weight by the amount that it changes your total weight and then commit it to memory. It will remain the same until the next turn, then the weight of each resouce will be set to a new random number.

  • You win the game by filling your bag to the exact weight that the order asks for. You lose the game if your total weight goes above the weight that the order asks for.
